# Product Vision

## What Nexus Is

Nexus is a recovery-focused EMR, billing, and executive operations platform for treatment organizations that need clinical, financial, and leadership workflows to stay aligned.

## Recovery-Center Context

Recovery organizations operate across tightly connected concerns:

- Clinicians deliver care and documentation.
- Billing teams convert documented care into clean, timely reimbursement.
- Leadership monitors capacity, outcomes, compliance posture, and operational risk.

Nexus is intended to support those interconnected workflows directly instead of treating them as disconnected tools.

## Why Three Role Workspaces

Nexus is organized around:

- Clinician
- Billing
- Executive / Leadership

This structure reflects how work is performed day-to-day and reduces drift toward generic, low-context patient CRUD tooling.

## Why Nexus Is More Specific Than a Generic EMR

Nexus prioritizes recovery operations, documentation quality, reimbursement readiness, and leadership visibility as one product direction.  
It is not designed as a generic “all settings” EMR abstraction.
