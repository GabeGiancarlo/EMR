# Roles And Workflows

## Clinician

Clinician workflows center on care delivery and clinical documentation:

- care delivery and session support
- charting and clinical notes
- treatment planning updates
- client progress tracking
- alerts and task follow-through
- forms and document completion

## Billing

Billing workflows center on reimbursement execution:

- insurance and policy details
- authorization lifecycle management
- claim creation and submission
- denials and appeals handling
- payments and balance tracking
- billing work queues and readiness checks

## Executive / Leadership

Leadership workflows center on operational and financial visibility:

- revenue and claim-health trends
- census and capacity status
- documentation compliance posture
- staff activity and workload visibility
- operational health signals
- outcomes and risk indicators
