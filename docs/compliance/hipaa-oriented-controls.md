# HIPAA-Oriented Controls

## Scope

This document describes planned controls for future compliance readiness.  
It is not a compliance certification and does not imply current full HIPAA implementation.

## Planned Controls

- access control and identity lifecycle management
- authentication and session/token controls
- authorization with role and organization/facility scope
- audit logging for sensitive activity
- encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- encryption at rest for databases/backups
- PHI-safe logging and log retention governance
- backup and recovery planning
- least-privilege operational access
- breach-response planning and incident runbooks
- vendor and dependency compliance review

## Current Status

- Controls are partially scaffolded in code/config areas.
- Full implementation, verification, and policy enforcement are still required before production use.
